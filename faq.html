Frequently Asked Questions

Eligibility

Am I eligible to apply?

  • Applicants must have DACA, TPS or be undocumented, meaning they currently do not have lawful status in the US
  • High GPA
  • Taking challenging level high school classes
  • Driven to help others in the community or through leadership positions

Do I have to be a current high school student to apply?

No. Recent high school graduates, currently enrolled undergraduates and transfer students (community college or 4-year college) are also welcome to apply.

Can this scholarship be applied toward graduate school?

No. Golden Door Scholarships may only be applied to undergraduate degree programs.

Are scholars expected to maintain a minimum gpa in college?

Yes, all scholars are expected to maintain at least a 3.0 GPA each semester.

Do I have to go attend a partner school to recieve the scholarship?

We support students at other accredited four year institutions on a case-by-case basis. We are also always developing relationships with new partner schools.
